## For Merchants

**Core promise:** Tell us how many customers you need. We deliver.

### Pain → Solution Framing

| Pain Point | Push Message (v5.0) |
|-----------|---------------------|
| "I don't know if creators actually work" | "Every customer triple-checked: QR + Claude Vision + geo-match. You pay only for who walks through your door." |
| "It's too expensive / risky" | "$0 Pilot for the first 10 Williamsburg coffee shops. AI delivers, or it's free." |
| "I don't have time to manage this" | "Tell us how many customers you need. Our AI matches the creator network, runs the campaign, verifies the visits." |
| "How do I know they'll show up?" | "AI-verified customer or it doesn't bill. Three-layer check in under 8 seconds." |

### Key Phrases to USE
- "AI-verified customer"
- "triple-checked: QR + Claude Vision + geo-match"
- "pay only for who walks through your door"
- "AI delivers, or it's free"
- "$0 Pilot"
- "outcome-based billing"

### Phrases to AVOID
- "influencer marketing" (outlawed)
- "marketplace" / "campaign marketplace" (outlawed — we're an agency)
- "brand awareness" (unmeasurable, off-strategy)
- "subscription" (use "outcome-based billing")
- "collab" / "partnership" (sounds casual, not accountable)
- "exposure" (worthless framing)
- "$19.99/mo" (outlawed except in grandfather-pricing legacy copy)

## For Creators

**Core promise:** Your results are your currency. Build them here.

### Pain → Solution Framing

| Pain Point | Push Message |
|-----------|-------------|
| "I'm too small for brand deals" | "No follower count needed. Start as Seed, earn your way up." |
| "I never know what I'll earn" | "Clear pay per campaign. Plus commission on every sale you drive — and milestone bonuses when you hit referral targets." |
| "Nobody tracks my real impact" | "Your Push Score proves your commercial value. It's your portfolio." |
| "How do I get better opportunities?" | "Higher score = higher tier = better pay + premium merchants." |

### Key Phrases to USE
- "earn from day one"
- "your Push Score"
- "level up" / "unlock"
- "commission on every sale"
- "performance is your portfolio"
- "creator network" (you're part of it — agency-network framing)

### Phrases to AVOID
- "side hustle" (diminishing)
- "free products" as the main pitch (Seed is the starting point, not the destination)
- "followers needed" / "minimum following" (Push's entire point is NO follower gate)
- "apply now!" (sounds like a job ad)
- "influencer" — use **creator**

## For Investors

**Core promise:** The first AI-powered customer acquisition agency — outcome-priced, creator-network-powered.

### Narrative Structure

1. **Problem:** Local merchants need customers on a schedule at a known cost. Ads are unmeasurable. DMing creators doesn't scale. Agencies charge retainers for guesswork.
2. **Insight:** AI can now do what agencies couldn't — match, run, and verify at the customer level in under 8 seconds. The agency model finally becomes outcome-priced.
3. **Solution:** AI-powered customer acquisition agency. Creator network on the supply side. Triple-checked verification (QR + Claude Vision + geo-match). Outcome-based billing ($0 Pilot + $500/mo min + $40/AI-verified customer).
4. **Moat:** Two-sided performance data that compounds. Every AI-verified customer sharpens matching. Competitors can copy the stack; they can't copy our beachhead data.
5. **Traction:** [Insert current numbers — AI-verified customers delivered, merchant repeat rate, creator completion rate]
6. **Ask:** [Funding amount] to saturate Williamsburg coffee, then expand category + ZIP by ZIP.

### Killer Lines for Investors

- "We don't sell software. We're the agency. AI is the operator."
- "Every AI-verified customer makes the matching sharper. That's a data moat no one can copy without running the same campaigns in the same ZIP."
- "In SaaS winter, we're not selling seats. We're selling customers — delivered and verified."
- "Shopify built the rails for e-commerce. We're the AI agency for local customer acquisition."

### Numbers to Always Lead With

- AI-verified customers delivered (the billable unit)
- Merchant repeat rate (THE PMF signal)
- Verification pass rate (three-layer check, under 8 seconds)
- Unit economics ($40/customer, target margin per campaign)
- Cost comparison vs alternatives ($500/mo + $40/customer vs $2-5K agency retainer with no verification)

## For Social Media

### Platform-Specific Tone

| Platform | Style | DO | DON'T |
|----------|-------|-----|--------|
| **Instagram** | Visual, aspirational but grounded | Show real creator content, real merchants, real AI-verified customer counts | Generic motivational quotes, stock photos |
| **TikTok** | Fast, authentic, behind-the-scenes | Day-in-the-life of Push campaigns, creator success stories, receipts | Overproduced, corporate feel |
| **LinkedIn** | Strategic, thought-leadership | Industry insights, founder journey, AI + agency market analysis | Humble-bragging, "thrilled to announce" |
| **Twitter/X** | Sharp, opinionated, concise | Hot takes on creator economy, verification data, contrarian views on "AI-assisted marketing" | Thread bait, engagement farming |

### Content Pillars

1. **Proof** — Real campaign results, real numbers, real merchants, real receipts
2. **Creator stories** — Progression journeys, tier unlocks, earning milestones
3. **Industry insight** — Why local acquisition is broken; why outcome-priced agency > retainer agency
4. **Behind the build** — Honest founder content, challenges, Williamsburg beachhead learnings
