# Push v5.0 GTM & Growth — Overview

## Launch Philosophy (v5.0)

**Single beachhead. Single category. Single ZIP cluster. Single quarter of saturation.**

Push v5.0 is a customer-acquisition agency for local merchants, priced on outcomes ($0 Pilot → $500/mo min + $40/verified customer on Performance). The GTM loop only works if we saturate one neighborhood with one category before anything else. Premature expansion kills density, and density is the product.

> **Never launch "a city." Never launch "Brooklyn coffee." We are launching Williamsburg coffee.**

---

## The v5.0 Beachhead — AUTHORITATIVE

| Axis | v5.0 Lock-in |
|------|--------------|
| **Category** | Coffee (only) |
| **Geography** | Williamsburg: ZIPs **11211 / 11206 / 11249** |
| **Time window** | 60 days of saturation (Week 1–8) |
| **Adjacent ZIP expansion** | Greenpoint (11222), then Bushwick (11237 / 11206) — only after Williamsburg saturation proves unit economics |
| **Category expansion** | Frozen until coffee beachhead proves unit economics end-to-end |

**What "saturation" means:**
- 10+ coffee merchants live in Williamsburg by Week 4
- 20+ creators recruited in 11211 / 11249 by Week 4
- 80% of Pilot merchants converted to Performance by Week 12
- 3+ published case studies (Sey / Devocion / Partners) by Week 12

**v4 artifacts to ignore:** any reference to dessert, beauty, LES, Astoria, LA, SF, or multi-category cohorts is v4.x debt. If a document still says "Café + Dessert + Beauty," it is stale and must be updated to v5.0 before use.

## Merchant Recruitment Playbook (v5.0)

| Channel | Description | Expected conversion |
|---------|-------------|---------------------|
| **1. Founder cold walk-in** | Walk in during dead hours (2–4pm Tue/Wed/Thu) with printed pilot sheet. Ask for owner. Pitch Pilot ($0, we deliver 10 verified customers or full refund of setup cost). | ~40% (highest, zero cost) |
| **2. Personalized Instagram DM** | Build IG follower list from Williamsburg coffee tags (#williamsburgcoffee, @seycoffee, @devocioncoffee). DM owner with a specific reference to their shop. | ~10% |
| **3. Manager-referral bonus** | $100 Amex gift card for any signed intro from an existing merchant's manager. Activated once we have 5+ live merchants. | ~70% on warm intros |

## Launch Experiments (v5.0 — run every 2 weeks)

Every experiment has a control group of 5 and a treatment group of 5, runs for 14 days, and reports into the public Williamsburg dashboard.

| # | Experiment | Control | Treatment | Metric |
|---|------------|---------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | **Merchant pricing positioning** | Current Performance pitch | Alternative pitch (outcome-first vs. price-first) | Pilot → Performance conversion % |
| 2 | **Creator compensation model** | Fixed $20 base per campaign | Variable 3% commission on verified customers | Creator completion rate + CAC |
| 3 | **Verification threshold (geo match)** | 200m geo radius pass window | 180m vs. 250m comparison | Verified-customer false-positive rate |

## Week 12 Saturation Go/No-Go (v5.0)

| Metric | Target | Interpretation |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Williamsburg coffee merchants live | **10+** | ✅ GO |
| Pilot → Performance conversion | **80%+** | ✅ GO |
| Verified customers delivered (total) | **200+** | ✅ GO |
| Creator completion rate | **85%+** | ✅ GO |
| Case studies published | **3** | ✅ GO |
| False-positive verification rate | **< 2%** | ✅ GO |
| Merchant NPS | **> 7/10** | ✅ GO |

**Decision logic:**
- ✅ **GO**: Begin Greenpoint (11222) in Week 13 using identical playbook
- ⚠️ **ITERATE**: One metric weak → extend Williamsburg saturation by 2 weeks, fix the weak loop
- ❌ **PIVOT**: No Performance conversion, or false-positive > 5% → stop expansion, revisit verification model or pricing positioning with user before proceeding

## Expansion Triggers (v5.0)

**New ZIP** (Greenpoint → Bushwick) only unlocks when Williamsburg coffee shows:
- 10+ merchants live on Performance
- 80% Pilot → Performance conversion
- 3 published case studies
- Verification false-positive rate < 2%
- Dashboard live at `/neighborhoods/williamsburg-coffee`

**New category** (anything beyond coffee) is **frozen** until Greenpoint + Bushwick also saturate on coffee. Do not reintroduce dessert or beauty without explicit user approval. No category experiments before Week 20.

---

## Retention Design (v5.0)

### Merchant Side
- Outcome-based pricing (no verified customers → no charge beyond $500/mo floor)
- Monthly ROI statement (auto-generated, emailed)
- Real-time dashboard at `/neighborhoods/williamsburg-coffee`
- One-click "run another campaign" (habit loop)
- Case-study features (social proof + press)

### Creator Side
- 24h fast payout (Venmo / Zelle)
- Push Score tier progression (Seed → Partner)
- Limited-seat campaigns (FOMO)
- Standby queue (near-miss)
- Non-portable score (loss aversion)
