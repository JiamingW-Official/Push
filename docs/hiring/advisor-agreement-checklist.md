## Screening & diligence

- [ ] Candidate passed screening rubric in `advisor-sourcing-playbook.md` §3 (avg ≥ 4.0, no single score ≤ 2).
- [ ] Two independent evaluators agreed within ±0.5 on the average score.
- [ ] References checked (≥2, at least one peer and one former founder/CEO they've advised).
- [ ] Background check run (for grants ≥ 0.3%) — result `Clear` or approved exception.
- [ ] Public-record search: no pending litigation, SEC enforcement, or regulatory action.
- [ ] Credit-adjacent check waived (not applicable to advisory engagements; do not run).

## Conflict of interest

- [ ] Conflict-of-interest disclosure signed (use Push standard COI form).
- [ ] Cross-check against cap table (`docs/week-0/legal/equity/granted/`) — candidate is not already a Push shareholder in another capacity.
- [ ] Cross-check against push-strategy §competition direct-competitor list — candidate has no active employment / advisory role with a direct competitor.
- [ ] If candidate has other advisor engagements, list them here with a statement that no fiduciary conflict exists:
  - Engagement 1: _______________________
  - Engagement 2: _______________________
  - Engagement 3: _______________________
- [ ] No active NDA with a third party that would be breached by advisory scope of work.

## Agreement scope

- [ ] IP assignment scope agreed in writing. Default: all work product created in the course of or relating to the advisory engagement is assigned to Push, Inc. Exclusions (pre-existing IP) listed on Exhibit A:
  - Excluded IP #1: _______________________
  - Excluded IP #2: _______________________
- [ ] Confidentiality term: 24 months post-termination; survives vesting.
- [ ] Non-solicitation: 12 months post-termination of Push employees, contractors, customers, and creators.
- [ ] Non-compete: **not included** (per NY SHRL and FTC 2024 guidance; see `advisor-sourcing-playbook.md` §6).
- [ ] Governing law: Delaware (corporate) with NY state venue for employment-adjacent disputes — confirmed by counsel.

## Compensation & vesting

- [ ] Comp structure confirmed: ______% RSA (within 0.25–0.5%), 2-year monthly vest, 6-month cliff, **no cash**, **no acceleration on single-trigger change-of-control**, double-trigger acceleration permitted.
- [ ] Purchase price = $0.00001/share at fair market value on grant date (per `RSA_TEMPLATE.md`).
- [ ] Repurchase right on termination-for-cause documented; default: Push may repurchase unvested shares at purchase price.
- [ ] 83(b) election guide delivered to advisor in writing; 30-day deadline flagged.
- [ ] FMV determination aligned to the most recent 409A valuation or board-approved FMV.

## Corporate formality

- [ ] Board consent drafted in Clerky referencing this grant.
- [ ] Cap table updated (Carta / Pulley / spreadsheet) with pending-grant note; finalised after counter-signature.
- [ ] Board approval obtained and recorded (minutes or written consent filed).
- [ ] Pro-forma dilution check: post-grant total advisor pool remains below 2.0% fully-diluted (board guardrail).

## Execution

- [ ] Advisor agreement generated via Clerky using the FAST-based template.
- [ ] Agreement attachments present: Exhibit A (excluded IP), Exhibit B (scope of services), Exhibit C (comp summary).
- [ ] Both parties counter-signed via Clerky e-signature.
- [ ] Copy of fully executed agreement filed at `docs/week-0/legal/equity/granted/<YYYY-MM-DD>-<advisor-name>.md` (includes reference to this checklist).
- [ ] Copy filed in Clerky vault and Push shared legal drive.

## Announcement & onboarding

- [ ] Announced internally (scope, time commitment, not equity percentage) to the founding team within 5 business days.
- [ ] Announced externally only with advisor's written consent — template language reviewed by advisor before publication.
- [ ] Advisor onboarded:
  - [ ] Product brief (v5.2 positioning + ConversionOracle deep-dive) delivered.
  - [ ] Data room access granted (least-privilege).
  - [ ] Slack workspace invite sent; advisor placed in `#advisors` channel only.
  - [ ] Calendar link exchanged (Cal.com / Calendly).
  - [ ] Monthly 45-min review slot booked for the next 6 months.
- [ ] First deliverable scoped (e.g., ConversionOracle threat-model review by Week 2 of engagement).

## Retention & compliance

- [ ] Retention-until set in the engagement record: 7 years post-termination of advisory engagement.
- [ ] Advisor added to compliance roster for quarterly conflict-of-interest re-attestation.
- [ ] Off-boarding checklist drafted (to be invoked on termination): revoke Slack, revoke data room, repurchase notice (if applicable), cap-table update, final Form 3921 / 3922 / W-9 handling per counsel.
