## Compliance Notes

All outreach operated by Push, Inc. must comply with the following. Tracker owner (Jiaming) certifies adherence by initialling the Day-7 send log.

1. **NY State Pay Transparency (LL-32-2022).** Any advertisement or communication that qualifies as a job / advisory-role posting in New York state must include a good-faith compensation range. Push's disclosed advisor compensation is **"0.25–0.5% equity over a 2-year vest, 6-month cliff, no cash."** This exact language (or a direct equivalent) must appear in every outreach message, verbal pitch, and offer communication. Absence of the range in a single message is treated as a compliance incident and logged.
2. **Equal Employment Opportunity (Title VII, ADA, ADEA, NY SHRL).** Advisor selection criteria must be documented and objective; see §1 of `advisor-sourcing-playbook.md` (ICP) and §3 (screening rubric). No target may be added, removed, or down-prioritised because of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age (40+), disability, pregnancy, genetic information, sexual orientation, gender identity, or any other protected class under federal, NY state, or NYC law.
3. **LinkedIn InMail Compliance (LinkedIn User Agreement §8.2, CAN-SPAM 15 U.S.C. §7701).** No mass-blasting; each InMail must be individually personalized. Sender identifies as "Jiaming — founder, Push, Inc." Each message contains an opt-out line ("If this isn't a fit, reply 'remove' and I'll not follow up."). Do not repurpose the same body more than twice per archetype per calendar week.
4. **Record Retention.** For non-hire candidates, retain outreach records (message copy, response, rationale) for **12 months** from last contact date (EEOC 29 CFR §1602 recommendation). For hires, retain for **7 years** including advisor agreements, board consents, and 83(b) election documentation.
5. **Conflicts & Cap Table.** Before first send, Jiaming runs `docs/week-0/legal/equity/granted/` cross-check + existing-advisor list: no target may be engaged who (a) already appears on Push's cap table, (b) is an active advisor/employee to a direct competitor (see `push-strategy` §competition), or (c) is the subject of a pending NDA with Push on the same subject matter.
6. **Anti-spam opt-out handling.** Any recipient that replies "remove", "unsubscribe", "not interested", or an equivalent is immediately added to `docs/hiring/do-not-contact.md` (TODO: Jiaming — create this file on first opt-out). Do-not-contact list is checked before any outreach, including re-engagement attempts ≥6 months later.
7. **Data handling.** Candidate PII (email, phone, home address if disclosed) is stored only in this tracker and the outreach archive; not shared with third parties, not pasted into public LLM sessions, not forwarded outside the founding team without the candidate's written consent.
